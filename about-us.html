Our vision is to establish India as the world's leading technology ecosystem

Nasscom represents the voices of leaders, researchers and future of India's $247 billion technology industry. 3000+ members access the power of collaboration across a network deeply embedded with players across the entire spectrum of industry — from startups to multinationals.

We partner with industry, government and academia to help build India into a global hub for technological products, services, talent and impact.
