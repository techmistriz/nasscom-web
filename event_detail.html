Africa Tech Festival, Expression of Interest

REGISTER HERE

Helping Indian technology businesses find new, untapped markets is one of the common goals shared by nasscom and Govt of India. The African region offers tremendous digital opportunities for the Indian tech industry that are currently under-utilized. To aid Indian technology companies establish and expand in the African continent, nasscom with the support from MeitY is organizing India Pavilion at the Africa Tech Fest (https://tmt.knect365.com/africa-tech-festival/) from 13th - 16th November in Cape Town. The event is a great way to showcase your offering, stay up to date on the African tech scene and interact with decision makers across sectors from sub-regions like South, East, West and Central Africa.

13-16 November, 2024
9am onwards

India Pavillion, Africa Tech Fest,
Cape Town, South Africa

Why should you participate?

Key Benefits

  • Present and brand yourself at the Africa Tech Show
  • Meet influential decision-markers shoping Africa's tech landscape.
  • Network and identity potential partners for your business.
  • Attend the conference and gain valuable insights from industry expert.
  • Discover latest trends and innovations in tech and how they're impacting Africa's ecosystem.
  • Present your business ideas to Africa's biggest community of tech leaders through pitch competitions.
  • Participate in additional nasscom bespoke programs, develop an understanding of other African markets and develop key contacts.
  • Leverage brand nasscom and the credibility it can extend to your business.
OPTIONS AND FEES

Option 1: Participate as an Exhibitor
Approx. INR 3,00,000 + GST / Company

  • A pod at the nasscom - India Pavilion
  • One full access pass
  • One exhibitor only pass.
  • Access to lead capturing tool
  • Participation in road shows & other programs and networking opportunities organized by nasscom

Option 2: Participate as a Delegate
Approx. INR 1,10,000 + GST / Participant

  • One full access pass
  • One exhibitor only pass.
  • Participation in road shows & other programs and networking opportunities organized by nasscom
